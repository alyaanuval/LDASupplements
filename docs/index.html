Preface

It is important to us that the reader has accessible resources that can support their learning when using the Loss Data Analytics book. Supplements for Loss Data Analytics aims to assist readers as they use the book.

This supplementary material includes:

  • a glossary,
  • a table of distributions and
  • conventions for consistent notation.
